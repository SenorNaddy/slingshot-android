Slingshot-android

Download .zip Download .tar.gz View on GitHub

A Broadband usage tracker for people who have Slingshot Broadband in New Zealand. It provides information on your data cap remaining, and total usage for the month, both on peak and off peak.

For AYCE (Unlimited) usage app will display current usage for that day (not total).

Users with iTalk will have their remaining iTalk minutes displayed.

This application is not developed by or associated with CallPlus or Slingshot. It simply accesses their API.

Your username and password are stored for the sole purpose of authenticating against the Slingshot API, and all communications with Slingshot are handled over HTTPS to protect your username and password over the network.
